Product Details

The Gildan Ultra Cotton Tee is a classic, comfortable t-shirt. It's made of 100% cotton, and comes in a variety of colors. The Gildan Ultra Cotton Tee has a seamless double-needle 2.2 cm collar, taped neck and shoulders, and double needle sleeves and bottom hems for extra durability. Ultra Cotton is also a slightly softer material, because the cotton is thicker and heavier and spun tighter. Even their labels are different! Ultra Cotton has a satin label, whereas Heavy Cotton has a tearable label. The heavy cotton sizing ranges from S to 3XL, while the ultra cotton's sizing runs from S to 5XL.
